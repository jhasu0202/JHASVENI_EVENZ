Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an event?
You can visit the Events page, select an event you like, choose your plan (Standard, Premium, or VIP), and confirm your booking.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the event. Refunds will be processed within 3–5 business days.
How can I view my receipts?
Go to your Profile and click on the Receipts tab to see all your past payments and bookings.
What payment methods are supported?
We support credit/debit cards, UPI, and PayPal for quick and secure transactions.
